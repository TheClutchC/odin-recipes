Bacon Mac & Cheese

Description

Wow. Just wow. How amazingly beautiful this dish is to look at and to eat. Wow. Wow wow wow. I won't waste your time any longer. Here's what you do.


Ingredients

  • 6 Slices Bacon, Chopped
  • Half Cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 Pound Macaroni, Cooked according to package directions
  • 5 Tbsp Butter
  • One-third Cup flour
  • 3 ½ Cups of milk
  • Salt and Pepper To taste
  • ¼ Cup Shredded Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 Cup Shredded Gruyere Cheese
  • 1 Cup Shredded cheddar
